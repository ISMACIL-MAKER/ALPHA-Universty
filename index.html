About Us

Welcome to Alpha University Burao.

Alpha University Burao, is part of branches for Alpha University in Somaliland, and Burao Branch is an indigenous private university established in late 2014 in Burao, with the aim of playing a significant role in the countrys social and health sector development goal. Currently, the university is providing undergraduate and post graduate degree as well.
